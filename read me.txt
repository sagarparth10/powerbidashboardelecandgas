AIM:
Data Analysis Case Study-

Edison Utilities PLC failed job reports for exploration and deriving the reason for more failures.


Steps to Replicate the solution:

1. Use PowerBI desktop app in Windows laptop

4. explore around the buttons,columns and points on line.
